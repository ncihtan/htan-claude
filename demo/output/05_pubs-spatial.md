# Search for HTAN publications about spatial transcriptomics from 2024

---

Here are **7 HTAN publications** about spatial transcriptomics from 2024:

1. **DBiTplus** (Enninful et al.) — Integrative method for spatial transcriptome + proteome mapping on the same tissue section. *(Research Square & bioRxiv)*
   - DOI: [10.1101/2024.11.07.622523](https://doi.org/10.1101/2024.11.07.622523)

2. **Breast cancer subtypes and lineages** (Iglesia et al.) — Multi-omic (bulk, single-cell, spatial) analysis of chromatin accessibility and transcriptional dynamics across breast cancer subtypes. *Nature Cancer*
   - DOI: [10.1038/s43018-024-00773-6](https://doi.org/10.1038/s43018-024-00773-6)

3. **Tumour evolution in 2D and 3D space** (Mo et al.) — 131 tumour sections across 6 cancer types profiled by Visium ST, snRNA-seq, and CODEX. *Nature*
   - DOI: [10.1038/s41586-024-08087-4](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41586-024-08087-4)

4. **Fallopian tube precursors to HGSOC** (Kader et al.) — Integrated high-plex imaging and spatial transcriptomics of ovarian cancer precursors. *bioRxiv*
   - DOI: [10.1101/2024.09.25.615007](https://doi.org/10.1101/2024.09.25.615007)

5. **Imputing protein abundance in multiplex imaging** (Kirchgaessner et al.) — Methods for addressing limited marker panels in spatial proteomics/transcriptomics. *bioRxiv*
   - DOI: [10.1101/2023.12.05.570058](https://doi.org/10.1101/2023.12.05.570058)

6. **Bone marrow niche biogeography** (Bandyopadhyay et al.) — scRNA-seq + proteomic imaging to map non-hematopoietic cell spatial organization in human bone marrow. *Cell*
   - DOI: [10.1016/j.cell.2024.04.013](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.cell.2024.04.013)
